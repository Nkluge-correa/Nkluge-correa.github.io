Ethical Problem-Solving (EPS) is a framework to promote the development of safe and ethical artificial intelligence. EPS is divided into an evaluation stage (performed via **Algorithmic Impact Assessment** tools) and a recommendation stage (the **WHY-SHOULD-HOW** method).
